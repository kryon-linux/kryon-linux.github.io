Frequently Asked Questions

Is the installation difficult?
✚

Not at all! We've created a graphical installer that's as simple as Ubuntu's or Windows'. An expert mode is also available for advanced users.

Will my Windows programs work?
✚

Many Windows applications and games work perfectly thanks to tools like Wine and Proton (built into Steam). It's always a good idea to check compatibility on sites like ProtonDB.

Where can I find help?
✚

Our community on Discord and our forums are very active. You can find the links in the footer. We also invite you to check out our public documentation.
